BEGAAA info


Bega Falls is located in Barangay Mabuhay in the town of Prosperidad, 
the capital of Agusan del Sur province, Philippines. Legend has it that a beautiful diwata (~ fairy) 
seduces men and lures them to the falls. Those who were enticed by the diwata were never again found. 
The falls got its name from the Visayan word biga which means to flirt in English. Whether the legend were true or not,
anyone who sees this falls will definitely be charmed by its beauty.

Address: Brgy. Mabuhay, Prosperidad, Agusan del Sur, Philippines.
